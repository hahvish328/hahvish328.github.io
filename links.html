Why the 2018 Honda Civic LX is the best thing produced by humans

It's fun

As the proud owner of a 2018 Honda Civic LX, the thing is fun to drive. It's got 158 horsepower, which isn't much. However, it's lighter than a BMW M3 by over a THOUSAND POUNDS. That means 0-60 miles an hour still comes in under seven seconds. Not only is it fast in a straight line, it takes corners like a beast. The low weight and excellent suspension geometry make the car handle like it's on rails.
